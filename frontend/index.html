About Quick Bite Logistics

Quick Bite is a state-of-the-art logistics and route optimization platform. By leveraging advanced computer science graph theory and pathfinding algorithms (such as Dijkstra's shortest path solver), Quick Bite maps nodes, calculates weight values, and relaxes routes to dispatch delivery agents efficiently.

Our platform separates administrative consoles from client panels. Admins manage agent states, add locations, and inspect business charts, while clients place food checkouts, configure home nodes, and watch delivery scooters tracking live along the optimized pathways.
